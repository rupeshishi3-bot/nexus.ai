💡 How Currency Conversion Works

Currency conversion uses exchange rates — the price of one currency in terms of another. These rates fluctuate based on economic indicators, interest rates, inflation, political stability, and market sentiment. Our tool uses approximate reference rates for quick calculations.

🏦 Getting the Best Exchange Rate

Banks typically offer rates 1–3% worse than the mid-market rate. Forex cards and online platforms like Wise or Revolut often provide rates much closer to the mid-market rate. Always compare before large transfers — on ₹1 lakh, even a 0.5% difference is ₹500.
