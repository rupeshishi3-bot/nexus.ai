💡 Tips for Using This Calculator

  • Add all your subjects with the correct max marks (they don't all have to be 100).
  • Use different max marks for subjects with 50, 75, or 150-mark papers.
  • The overall score is a weighted average based on total marks across all subjects.
  • You can use this for any exam — school, college, entrance, or competitive exams.
