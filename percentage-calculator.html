📘 Three Ways to Use This Calculator

1. Percentage of a Number — Find X% of any number.

Result = (Percentage ÷ 100) × Number

Example: 15% of 2500 = (15 ÷ 100) × 2500 = 375

2. Percentage Change — Find how much something increased or decreased.

Change % = ((New − Old) ÷ Old) × 100

Example: From ₹1,000 to ₹1,200 = ((1200−1000) ÷ 1000) × 100 = +20% increase

3. X is What % of Y — Find what percentage one number is of another.

Percentage = (X ÷ Y) × 100

Example: 75 out of 300 = (75 ÷ 300) × 100 = 25%

💡 Pro tip: Use "% Change" to compare exam scores across terms, or salary hike percentages. Use "X is what %" to find your score percentage from marks obtained.
