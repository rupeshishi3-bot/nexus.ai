💡 How to Save as PDF

Click "Print / Download PDF", then in the print dialog choose "Save as PDF" as the destination (available in Chrome, Edge, Firefox). This gives you a clean PDF invoice you can email to your client.

🧾 GST on Invoices

If you're a GST-registered business, your invoice must include your GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, and the tax amount split into CGST + SGST (intra-state) or IGST (inter-state). This simple invoice generator is ideal for small freelancers and service providers who don't need the full GST format yet.
