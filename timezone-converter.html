🌍 Understanding UTC Offsets

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the world's time standard. Every time zone is defined as UTC + or − some offset. India Standard Time (IST) is UTC+5:30. Japan Standard Time (JST) is UTC+9. These offsets can shift during Daylight Saving Time (DST), which India does not observe.

💡 Scheduling Across Time Zones

A meeting at 10:00 AM IST is 4:30 AM UTC, 12:30 AM New York (EST), 9:30 PM previous day in Los Angeles (PST), and 1:30 PM in Dubai. Always double-check for DST transitions — the US, Europe, and Australia observe it while India, China, UAE, and Japan do not.

⚡ IST to Other Major Cities

IST (UTC+5:30) is unique in having a 30-minute offset. When it's noon in India: it's 6:30 AM in London, 1:30 AM in New York, 2:30 PM in Singapore, 3:30 PM in Tokyo, and 6:30 PM in Sydney (AEST).
