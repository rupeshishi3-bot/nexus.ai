🔑 Keyword Density

Keyword density matters for SEO and academic writing. A density of 1–2% for a target keyword is generally considered healthy for web content. Anything above 3% may be seen as keyword stuffing. Common stop words (the, a, is, in…) are excluded from the density chart.
