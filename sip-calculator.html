📐 SIP Formula

FV = P × ((1+r)ⁿ − 1) / r × (1+r)

Where P = Monthly amount, r = Monthly rate, n = Total months.

💡 The power of compounding: ₹10,000/month at 12% for 15 years grows to over ₹50 lakhs — on just ₹18 lakhs invested!
  • Equity mutual funds historically return 12–15% CAGR over long periods
  • Debt funds typically return 6–8% per year
  • Use 12% for aggressive and 8% for conservative projections
