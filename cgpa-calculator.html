📐 How the Conversion Works

Most Indian universities use a multiplier formula to convert CGPA to percentage. The most widely accepted formula (used by CBSE, Anna University, VTU, and many others) is:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

For example: A CGPA of 8.5 on a 10-point scale = 8.5 × 9.5 = 80.75%

⚠️ Note: Different universities may use different multipliers (9.0, 9.5, or custom formulas). Always check your university's official conversion policy.

For 4-point GPA scales (common in US universities and some Indian institutions), the formula is:

Percentage = (GPA / 4) × 100
