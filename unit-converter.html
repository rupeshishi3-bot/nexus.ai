📏 Length — Common References

1 foot = 12 inches = 30.48 cm. 1 mile = 1.609 km. 1 nautical mile = 1.852 km. The metre is the SI base unit, defined as the distance light travels in 1/299,792,458 of a second.

🌡️ Temperature — Formula Guide

°C → °F: multiply by 9/5, then add 32
°F → °C: subtract 32, multiply by 5/9
°C → K: add 273.15  |  K → °C: subtract 273.15

💾 Data Units

1 KB = 1,024 bytes. 1 MB = 1,024 KB. 1 GB = 1,024 MB. 1 TB = 1,024 GB. Note: storage manufacturers often use 1 KB = 1,000 bytes (SI), which is why a "1 TB" drive shows ~931 GiB in your OS.

⚡ Why Unit Conversions Matter

In 1999, NASA's Mars Climate Orbiter was lost because one team used metric units and another used imperial — a $327 million mistake. Getting unit conversions right is critical in engineering, medicine, cooking, and international trade.
