💼 How CTC is Structured

CTC (Cost to Company) is the total amount a company spends on an employee. Your actual in-hand salary is lower because of mandatory deductions:

  • PF (Provident Fund): 12% of Basic — deducted from both employee and employer
  • Gratuity: ~4.81% of Basic — employer contribution, not monthly payout
  • TDS: Tax Deducted at Source — approximately 10% on gross for mid-range salaries
💡 A ₹12 LPA CTC doesn't mean ₹1 lakh/month in hand — typical in-hand is ₹75,000–₹82,000 depending on deductions and tax regime.
