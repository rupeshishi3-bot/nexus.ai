📅 How Age Is Calculated

This calculator uses precise calendar arithmetic — accounting for leap years, varying month lengths, and the exact number of days. The result is your age in complete years, months, and remaining days, not just a rough estimate.

🌍 Age Around the World

In most countries, age is calculated from birth date. In South Korea, a traditional system adds 1 year at birth and another on January 1st, making someone 1–2 years "older" than their international age. Since 2023, South Korea officially adopted the international standard for legal and administrative purposes.
