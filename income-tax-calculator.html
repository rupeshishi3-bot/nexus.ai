📋 New Regime Tax Slabs (AY 2025-26)

  • Up to ₹3,00,000 — Nil
  • ₹3,00,001 – ₹6,00,000 — 5%
  • ₹6,00,001 – ₹9,00,000 — 10%
  • ₹9,00,001 – ₹12,00,000 — 15%
  • ₹12,00,001 – ₹15,00,000 — 20%
  • Above ₹15,00,000 — 30%
💡 Rebate u/s 87A: If your taxable income is ≤ ₹7 lakhs under new regime, tax is NIL. Choose old regime if your deductions (80C, HRA, etc.) exceed ₹3.75 lakhs.

4% Health & Education Cess is added on the calculated tax for both regimes.
